Reason for Selling

I love this bike for solo adventuring. I can ride single track with 250s or on the highway with bigger bikes. It’s all good.

The one thing I can't do is bring a passenger. I'm finding I miss that ability. It's something I really enjoyed on previous bikes. If I could keep this and have something bigger too, I would. I've worked hard to make this the perfect setup. I hope it serves someone else as well as it has me.
